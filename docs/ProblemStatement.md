# SmartTraffic

## Challenge Chosen

Event-Driven Congestion (Planned & Unplanned)

## Objective

Develop an AI-powered system capable of forecasting event-related traffic congestion and recommending proactive traffic management strategies.

## Inputs

- Event Type
- Location
- Date
- Time
- Expected Attendance
- Weather Conditions

## Outputs

- Predicted Traffic Demand
- Congestion Risk Level
- Affected Areas
- Police Deployment Recommendation
- Barricading Recommendation
- Diversion Route Suggestions
